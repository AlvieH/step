Hi! I'm Alvin Hermans, and I do funky computer things on the internet. I believe in making our devices approachable and fun to use, without compromising on privacy.
Right now I'm a Computer Science and Cognitive Science student at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor, where I mostly run Human-Computer Interaction research projects at the Computational HCI Lab and work in theater productions for University Productions. I'm also currently interning at Google through the STEP program, where I'm assisting with Google AI computer vision technology to detect landmarks.
In my spare time you can find me tinkering with retro electronics, baking, knitting, or taking naps (sometimes all at once).
